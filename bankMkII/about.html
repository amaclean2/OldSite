4mal is a site designed to help you keep track of your expenses. Enter your bank accounts and 4mal will tell you everything you need to know about your spending. Whether you want to know if you have spent too much money on Starbucks, or how long you have until you run out of funds, 4mal will help keep track of this for you. This is just the beginning and we are still working out the kinks. At the moment we are not able to accept real bank accounts but you can play with the site as if there were, and please remember for the safety of all your accounts, please use seperate usernames and passwords for all your accounts.
